Princeton Undergraduate Law Review (PULR)

Begun in 2014, the Princeton Undergraduate Law Review is dedicated to providing clear, accessible, and incisive analysis of legal and policy issues. We are staffed entirely by a group of passionate, legal-minded Princeton undergraduates, and we accept submissions from undergraduate and graduate students both inside and out of the Princeton community. Our goal is to provide both our fellow undergraduates and the community at large with high quality discussion of the impact and workings of specific laws, rulings, and legal concepts in a way that emphasizes their direct relevance to the lives of our readership.
